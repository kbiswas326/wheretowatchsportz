Key Tournaments

What to Watch

🏆
ICC Cricket World Cup (ODI)
Held every 4 years. Next edition: 2027. Watch on JioCinema, Hotstar, Sky Sports, Willow TV, Fox Sports.
⚡
ICC T20 World Cup
Every 2 years. Fast-paced 20-over format. JioCinema, ESPN+, Sky Sports.
🏏
Indian Premier League (IPL)
Every March–May. India's flagship T20 league. JioCinema FREE in India. Willow TV (USA). Sky Sports (UK).
🎖
The Ashes
England vs Australia. Test cricket's greatest rivalry. Sky Sports (UK), Fox Sports (AU), Hotstar (IN).
🏅
ICC Champions Trophy
Elite ODI tournament for top 8 nations. JioCinema, Sky Sports, Willow TV.
🌙
ICC World Test Championship
Two-year cycle culminating in a Final. All Test series count toward standings. JioCinema, Sky Sports.
