India Women kept their ICC Women's T20 World Cup 2026 semi-final qualification on track with a five-wicket win over Bangladesh at Old Trafford, Manchester on June 25 — though the performance was far from convincing.

## Match Summary — India 139/5 beat Bangladesh 136/8

Shafali Verma was the hero with the bat, blasting 53 off just 34 balls to give India a flying start to their chase of 137. Bangladesh had posted a competitive 136 for 8 on a flat pitch, with Juairiya Ferdous (33) and Nigar Sultana (32) providing the backbone of their innings. India's spinners, led by Radha Yadav (3-28), did the damage with the ball.

The chase was shakier than the scoreline suggests. India dropped three catches in the field, misfielded several times, and saw Harmanpreet Kaur and Jemimah Rodrigues both survive run-out chances before Rodrigues (26) accelerated to bring India home with 19 balls to spare.

## What's Next for India

India now face Australia in a crucial Group 1 encounter at Lord's on June 28 — effectively a knockout match for a semi-final berth. India sit two points clear of South Africa and Bangladesh in second place behind Australia.
