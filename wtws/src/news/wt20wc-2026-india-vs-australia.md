India Women and Australia Women are locked in one of the tournament's biggest clashes at Lord's on June 28 — a virtual knockout match with a Women's T20 World Cup 2026 semi-final place on the line.

## The Match

India Women posted 170 for 4 from their 20 overs, giving Australia a testing target. The chase is live, with Australia in the hunt but needing a strong finish to chase it down. Ellyse Perry and Ashleigh Gardner are at the crease for Australia, who need 53 from the remaining 32 balls as of the latest update.

## Why This Match Matters

Both India and Australia are fighting for a top-two finish in Group 1. Australia started the tournament as one of the favourites; India have been inconsistent but dangerous. The winner goes through to the semi-finals at The Oval on June 30. South Africa are also watching closely, having beaten Bangladesh 118/6 earlier today at Lord's.

## Semi-Final Schedule

- **1st Semi-Final:** The Oval, June 30
- **2nd Semi-Final:** The Oval, July 2
- **Final:** Lord's, July 5
