The Minnesota Lynx survived a late scare to beat the Washington Mystics 78-76 on June 24, with Natasha Howard delivering a season-high double-double of 21 points and 15 rebounds to keep Minnesota in the playoff picture.

## Game Summary

Howard was the standout performer on the night, posting a career-best 15 rebounds to go alongside her 21 points as the Lynx held on for a nerve-wracking two-point win. The Mystics had multiple chances to level in the final minutes but couldn't convert.

The win was tight throughout, with neither team managing to build more than a single-figure lead at any point in the fourth quarter. Howard's presence in the paint was the difference — dominating the boards on both ends when the game was at its most critical.

## Standings Watch

The Lynx remain in contention in the competitive Western Conference standings heading into the second half of the regular season.
