The Indiana Fever improved to 7-5 on the season with a hard-fought 114-106 overtime win over the Chicago Sky on June 11, in what was one of the most entertaining games of the 2026 WNBA Commissioner's Cup.

## Game Summary

The Fever needed overtime to put away the Sky in a game that swung multiple times. Aliyah Boston and Caitlin Clark both delivered when it mattered most, combining to keep Indiana ahead in the extra period. The Sky gave everything but couldn't find the plays to force a second overtime.

The win was Indiana's fourth consecutive victory and pushed them to 7-5 — well placed for a playoff push heading into the second half of the season.

The 2026 WNBA season features 15 teams, with the Portland Fire and Toronto Tempo among the new expansion franchises. Games are distributed across Disney, NBCUniversal and Amazon Prime Video.
