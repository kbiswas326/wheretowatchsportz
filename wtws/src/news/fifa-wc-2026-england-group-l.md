England finished top of Group L at the 2026 FIFA World Cup, with Harry Kane and Jude Bellingham on the scoresheet in a 2–0 win over Panama on June 27.

## Group L Results

England's final group fixture was never in doubt, with Kane converting a first-half penalty and Bellingham adding a second after the break to seal a comfortable victory. The Three Lions head into the Round of 32 in confident form, with two of their most important players finding their scoring boots at just the right time.

Colombia finished second in the group after drawing 0–0 with Portugal, who also advanced. Panama were eliminated.

## What It Means for the Knockouts

England will now face a Round of 32 clash determined by the bracket draw. They are considered one of the favourites to go deep in the tournament, with their squad containing some of Europe's finest in-form players.
